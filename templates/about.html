What is the need for Cybersecurity ?

Internet has several fruitful uses but not everyone uses it ethically .There have been numerous cases of website hacks, server breaches, leakage of PSI (Personal Sensitive Information) and overall financial damage to the companies facing these cyber threats. Cyber attacks come in various forms, each with its own tactics, techniques, and objectives.
Cybersecurity helps maintain privacy by ensuring that individuals' personal information remains confidential and is only accessed by authorized parties. Without adequate cybersecurity measures, personal privacy can be compromised through data breaches or unauthorized surveillance.
